Q/A based on Covid-19

What is Covid-19 ?

Covid-19 is a disease caused which is a decent of the family of SARS-CoV-2. It was the first found on 31 December, 2019 in the cluster formed in Wuhan a known city of China.

What are the symptoms of Covid-19 ?                                     

The commonly seen symptom in humans of Covid-19 are


-- Fever

-- Dry Cough

-- Fatigue


The less commonly seen symptoms are mainly...


-- Dizziness

-- Diarrhea

-- Body Pain

-- Skin Rashes 

                even more...

The most risked people to get Covid-19 are....

The most risked people who are likely to get Covid-19 are
-- People age above 60 yrs old.
-- People undergoing surgery or taking medicines for injuries.
-- People having medical problems like cancer, obesity, etc.

Any long term effects in our body after Covid-19 gets affected ?

Our body might experience some symptoms of neurological symptoms and even fatigue...

WHO is working on it to find the period of time it may take to recover from. 

How to prevent ourselves from this if we don't know who is affected                                     

Stay safe by taking some simple precautions, such as physical distancing, wearing a mask, especially when distancing cannot be maintained, keeping rooms well ventilated, avoiding crowds and close contact, regularly cleaning your hands, and coughing into a bent elbow or tissue. Check local advice where you live and work. Do it all!

Also read the public advise page given by WHO.
