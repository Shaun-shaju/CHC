What are Travel Rules And Restrictions For Different Indian States in 2021?

Travel rules for Kerala in 2021

All passengers are required to adhere to a 14 day home quarantine. Travellers visiting Kerala for less than 7 days for business, official purposes, medical, law & order etc.. are exempted from the home quarantine. To enter Kerala, visitors require an entry pass that is available on Jagratha portal. Visitors need to disclose their purpose of visit, local accommodation details and contact information.

Travel rules for Maharashtra in 2021

People visiting Maharashtra from Gujarat, Delhi-NCR, Goa, Rajasthan and Kerala will have to produce a negative RTPCR test report on arrival, irrespective of the mode of travel. For passengers travelling by air, the test report should be within 72 hours prior to the flight.

Travel rules for Karnataka in 2021

People visiting the capital city of Karnataka, Bengaluru is required to carry a negative RT-PCR COVID-19 test report to enter the city. Visitors from the states of Maharashtra, Kerala, and Punjab were asked to produce their negative RT-PCR COVID-19 test report prior to entering Bangalore.

Travel rules for Gujrat in 2021

The government of Gujarat has announced that a negative COVID-19 RT-PCR test report will be mandatory for those arriving from other states.

Travel rules for Jammu & Kashmir in 2021

Visitors from all states will have to provide a negative RTPCR test report on arrival in the J&K.

Travel rules for Rajasthan in 2021

Travellers visiting tourist places such as Jaipur, Jodhpur and Udaipur from Punjab, Haryana, Madhya Pradesh and Gujarat to required to produce a COVID-19 negative report on arrival in the state of Rajasthan.

Travel rules for Tamil Nadu in 2021

All visitors require an e-pass that can be applied on the Tamilnadu state portal. It is mandatory for international and domestic passengers, except those from Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh and the neighbouring Puducherry union territory to have an e-pass to be allowed entry into the state.

Travel rules for Delhi in 2021

The covid-19 test is not required for passengers travelling to Delhi. However, the thermal screening will be done for all passengers upon arrival.

Which are the Indian states that have no travel restrictions for Domestic travelers?

Goa, Bihar, Himachal Pradesh, Andhra Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Punjab, Haryana and Jharkhand do not have any sort of travel restriction for domestic tourist and travellers.
